About Us

The SSN Entrepreneurship Development Cell “Lakshya” was formed a few years ago with a handful of enthusiasts and has evolved over its short lifespan into a powerful student body that boasts of 700+ members, all of whom are active participants in various entrepreneurial activities undertaken by Lakshya. Lakshya has always aimed at celebrating the evergreen spirit of entrepreneurship by conducting a plethora of events that kindles the entrepreneurial spirit within students. Ever since its inception it has had a profound impact on the lives and thinking of the “Young India”. Our E-cell lifted the prestigious E-week India trophy during the years 2010 and 2011 under the guidance of the National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN), a Dr. Romesh Wadhwani foundation, and were adjudged runners-up for the same during the years 2012 and 2013.


HOW DO I CONTRIBUTE

Students can contribute in several ways, by volunteering for events and social cause, helping the EDC acquire sponsors, prepare posters, be part of publicity team, etc. Also get in touch with other existing members to know more. You could start off by registering here with your contact details to be notified incase of any updates/events.

Social Entrepreneurship
Teach A School(TAS) is a year long social entrepreneurship event.
What do we do?
Volunteers from our college of all years and departments who are a part of Teach a school program contribute by providing mentorship to middle school students (6th-8th) from government schools across the city.

How do we do it?
English and mathematics are taught in an engaging and fun manner. Our volunteers organise interesting activities and prepare worksheets to teach concepts by captivating the attention of the students.

Who do we work with?
We take in government schools which are open to our help under the TAS umbrella every year. We have been associated with schools like Tiruporur Government school, Eechangadu Government School, Nandanam Higher Secondary School, Olcott Memorial School and Lady Sivasami Girls Higher Secondary School and associations like Tribal Hearts Foundation.

How do I join?
Students who would like to experience the joy of teaching and giving back to society are welcome to join the TAS family. Registrations are open at the beginning of each semester. Any interested student of SSN can register with TAS by either using the link in the website or the one on our Instagram/facebook handles.
