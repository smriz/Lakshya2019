AN IDEA CANNOT BE KILLED!

All is you need is an innovative idea which is completely feasible. Properly developed, the results could potentially change your life for the better. SSN Lakshya's 'The 32 Hour Startup Challenge' is an intense contest that tests participants on their ability to produce workable business models and implement timely manoeuvrings, which is key for any successful startup. Participants are continually mentored by professionals through the stipulated 32 hours to deliver their product. It isn't the coffee or Red Bull, but their passion to embody their idea that keeps the participants awake. Exciting prizes await the participants, so hang in there!
