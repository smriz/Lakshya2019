BRING OUT THE ENTREPRENEUR IN YOU!

Put your thinking caps on to attend this entrepreneurship event, where we challenge you to display your acumen and entrepreneurial skills through a series of innovative games.

Can you make a cost-efficient product out of a random selection of materials, along with an exemplary business model? How well-versed are you in your knowledge of the nuances of the current economy?

Encounter that and much more at Crazy Bazaar, the event that exemplifies entrepreneurial spirit on the campus.
