LITERALLY A MELA!

Similar to how "mela" is synonymous to fun and action, SSN Lakshya's annual extravaganza 'MELA' also lives up to the same reputation. Mela witnesses over 250 participants, who assume the role of entrepreneurs for the day by setting up stalls. The diverse variety of stalls never ceases to amaze one, right from food, art, fun games, photography, Mela has it all. It is a window where talent and creativity meet business.

By coming up with suitable business models, the Participants must capitalize on their talent and hence, steer their stalls towards financial success.
