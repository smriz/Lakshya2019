Campus Diaries – Memorandum of Understanding

Campus Diaries is India’s largest discovery and collaboration platform for students and universities. This portal is used by over 3 lakh students from over 1000 universities and schools across 5 countries. A startup based out of Bangalore, their mission is to empower the youth to create value for themselves, their communities and the world. Lakshya is thrilled to join hands with Campus Diaries and create a platform which is home to work, ideas and talents that can be nurtured and imbibed, keeping in line with the spirit of Entrepreneurship.

IIT Kharagpur – Memorandum of Understanding

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT-Kharagpur is a non-profit student organization, dedicated to the cause of promoting entrepreneurship among students. It invites various eminent entrepreneurs to deliver lectures to students about the joys and hardship of entrepreneurship. Guest lectures, case studies, workshops, knowledge camps and patent workshops are some of the activities conducted frequently to keep students involved in skills that are essential to an entrepreneur.

SSN Lakshya – Memorandum of Understanding

SSN Lakshya and the Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT Kharagpur joined hands to put up the Chennai edition of the Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive on the 13th of October, 2015 at the Justice Pratap Singh auditorium, SSN. The Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive is an initiative taken to promote entrepreneurship and encourage students across the country to embrace the concept. The EAD is conducted in 24 cities and the Chennai edition concluded amidst much grandeur with eminent personalities like Mr. Satya Prabakar – Founder and CEO at sulekha.com, Mr Venugopal – Chief Mentor at Startup Buddy delivering powerful speeches.
