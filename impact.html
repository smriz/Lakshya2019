Impact

The 32 Hour Startup marathon is a one of its kind venture that sets participants down an entrepreneurial path they’ve always aspired for and helps them become a part of the ever growing and exciting. As a trio of developer, designer and business strategist, each team will be pitching an idea, working on a business model, developing a working prototype inclusive of an inte rface and presenting the collective outcome of their efforts with guidance from accomplished mentors every step of the way.
