A BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT SIMULATION

'Udaan' is a fastpaced business simulation event conducted annually by SSN Lakshya. This event invigorates entrepreneurial spirits in students and brings it to action. It helps gives a clear insight into how to effectively manage a company. A stitch in time saves nine, is something that the event is based on.

A two-phase event, here, the students are randomly teamed up to form mock companies. They are given the basic materials from which they are expected to manufacture certain specified products, that correlate to real-time products. The teams are also internally grouped to perform various tasks such as managing, registering, finance, marketing, and striking up deals, to name a few. At the end of the stipulated time, two teams whose companies have fared well both in stocks and profits move to the next round where the companies go through an auction. Here, each company bids for rights such as property, material, labour, political connection, media. This fun-filled, creative event is an eye-opener for young, novice entrepreneurs.
